The Numerology Game

The Numerology Game is a mobile training game for people practicing numerology and astrology basics. Players solve randomly generated birthdays by choosing the correct Life Path Number, Eastern Zodiac, and Western Zodiac before the timer runs out.

The game is designed for focused practice sessions, local score history, sound feedback, and a simple mobile-first interface.
